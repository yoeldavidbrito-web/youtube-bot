Privacy Policy

Mage Arcane Gaming Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Mage Arcane Gaming operates a desktop workflow that creates short-form videos and uploads them as drafts for creator review before posting to social platforms.

Information We Use

The app may store API credentials, access tokens, generated video files, metadata, and local configuration files that are required to authenticate accounts and upload content on behalf of the authorized user.

How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate the creator with supported third-party platforms.
  • To generate, process, and upload draft videos requested by the creator.
  • To save local project state, logs, and review assets needed for quality control.

Data Sharing

Mage Arcane Gaming does not sell personal data. Information is only used locally or sent to the specific third-party services required to fulfill creator-requested actions, such as generating media or uploading drafts.

Local Storage

Tokens and generated assets may be stored locally on the creator's machine so the app can continue authorized workflows without repeated logins.

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep credentials and local files limited to the creator's environment. Users are responsible for securing access to their own device and accounts.

Contact

For privacy questions related to this app, contact the app owner through the TikTok Developer app listing contact details.
